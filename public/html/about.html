About Knock.

How to Use

Knock (Knowledge Unlock) is a simple application that helps you track the knowledge or skills you want to master. Here are the steps to use this application:

  1. Add Knowledge: Type the knowledge or skill you want to learn in the input field on the main page, then click the Post button. The new knowledge will be added to the list with the status Locked.
  2. Unlock Knowledge: Once you have mastered the knowledge, click the Unlock button on the knowledge item. Its status will change to Unlocked and it will be moved to the top of the list.
  3. Delete Knowledge: If you want to remove the knowledge from the list, click the Delete button.

This application also saves your data in Local Storage, so you don't have to worry about losing your data even if you close or refresh the page.
